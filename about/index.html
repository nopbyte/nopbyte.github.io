Short Bio

Projects

  • SEMIoTICS (Smart End-to-end Massive IoT Interoperability, Connectivity and Security:) 2019-today
    • I started working for this project in 2019, and I am looking forward to contribute to detection of computational resource abuse for software deployed on the platform.
  • AGILE (Adaptive & Modular Gateway for the IoT) 2015-2018:
    • I was the security work package leader and the main researcher at the University of Passau throughout the project.
    • One of the results from my effort spent in this project was an open source Access Control framework for the gateway. I also published a quantitative evaluation of the component (see the publications page)
  • COMPOSE (Collaborative Open Market to Place Objects at your Service) 2012-2015:
    • The project had 12 partners from industry and academia, and it was funded by the European Union FP7 programme for 3 years.
    • I worked as a research assistant and contributed to a security framework for a cloud-based marketplace for the IoT. Specifically, I created an Identity Management relying on a Cloud Foundry’s User Authentication Service
